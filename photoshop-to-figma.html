Photoshop migration

Move Photoshop designs into Figma

PSD2Figma helps designers migrate old Photoshop website mockups, landing pages, app screens and UI kits into Figma without rebuilding every layer by hand.

Install PSD2FigmaSee layer support

When Photoshop to Figma conversion helps

  • You have archived `.psd` files that need to become editable Figma work.
  • Your agency receives Photoshop source files but collaborates in Figma.
  • You want to modernize old landing pages or product pages without starting from a blank canvas.
  • You need a Figma version for dev handoff, comments or design-system cleanup.

What PSD2Figma preserves

Photoshop sourceFigma result
Groups and nested layersFrames/groups with matching hierarchy
Text layersEditable Figma text where possible
Images and smart objectsImage fills or image layers
Shadows, strokes and gradientsFigma effects and fills
