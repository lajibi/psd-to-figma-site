Common questions

Can Figma open PSD files?

Figma does not natively open PSD files. PSD2Figma adds a plugin workflow for importing Photoshop PSD files into editable Figma layers.

Is PSD2Figma free?

Yes. The free plan includes 3 conversions per month. Pro costs $6/month and unlocks unlimited conversions.

Are PSD files uploaded to PSD2Figma servers?

No. PSD2Figma is designed around local processing. The PSD is parsed in the plugin UI and converted into Figma objects without uploading your design content to our servers.

Does PSD2Figma preserve layers?

PSD2Figma recreates groups, image layers, text, shapes, masks and effects as editable Figma objects wherever possible. Very complex Photoshop-specific effects may still need review.

Can PSD2Figma export Figma back to PSD?

Yes. The plugin supports bidirectional conversion: PSD to Figma import and Figma to PSD export.

What happens if a Photoshop font is missing?

If the exact font is not available locally or in Figma, the plugin may map or substitute fonts. Always review typography after import.

Who should use Pro?

Use Pro if you convert PSD files repeatedly, migrate old Photoshop archives, or run agency/client workflows that need more than 3 conversions per month.
