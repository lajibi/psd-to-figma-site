When an online converter is enough

If you only need a rough visual reference and the PSD is not sensitive, an online converter may be acceptable. If you need editable layers or do not want to upload client files, use a Figma plugin workflow.

When PSD2Figma is the better fit

  • Client files or private design assets should stay local.
  • You want editable text, groups, effects and image layers in Figma.
  • You already work in Figma and want direct import into the current file.
  • You need repeat conversion for an agency or migration workflow.
